About Vishveshwarya Collage

Vishveshwarya Group of Institutions (VGI) was established in the year 2000 in order to impart high quality education to the students in the areas of Technology, Pharmacy, Management, Agriculture and various other courses. Since its inception, the college has continuously been ranked in the top colleges in the Uttar Pradesh State. Vishveshwarya Group of Institutions has been gratefully borrowed from the name of Sir Mokshagundam Vishveshwarya,Indiaâ€™s first civil Engineer.

The undergraduate and post graduate courses offered by Vishveshwarya Group of Institutions is governed by the following bodies:-
ALL INDIA COUNCIL OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION (AICTE)
PHARMACY COUNCIL OF INDIA (PCI)
FEDERATION OF INDIAN CHAMBERS OF COMMERCE & INDUSTRIES (FICCI)

The bachelors and masters programs in technology ,pharmacy and management are affiliated to the Dr. APJ ABDUL KALAM TECHNICAL UNIVERSITY where as the diploma programs in engineering are governed by the BOARD OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION, Uttar Pradesh. The college also offers Bachelors in Education program that is governed by CHAUDHARY CHARAN SINGH UNIVERSITY, Meerut, Uttar Pradesh.

Vishveshwarya Group of Institutions is located at Greater Noida phase-2 near Dadri at Ghaziabad-Bulandshahr highway. It is approximately 20 kilometers away from Ghaziabad city center and 8 kilometers from Pari Chowk, Greater Noida.

The college has a beautiful 65 acres huge campus with lush green lawns, and boasts of the most modern infrastructure including state of the art laboratories having cutting edge apparatus and equipment. Separate boarding and lodging facilities are available for boys and girls within the campus itself.
